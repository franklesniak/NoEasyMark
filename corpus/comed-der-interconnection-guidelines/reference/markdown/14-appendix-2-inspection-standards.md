# Appendix 2: Pre-Interconnect Inspection Standards

Before parallel operation of the renewable facility with the ComEd Distribution System, witness testing may be required and inspected by ComEd. The Interconnection Customer is responsible for providing qualified personnel who will complete all required tests. Witness testing is generally required for larger generators; however, ComEd reserves the right to require witness testing for each DER Interconnection.

Qualified testing personnel must perform tests on the Interconnection Customer's protective relaying prior to energizing on the ComEd System. Testing requirements will be evaluated and determined on a case-by-case basis by ComEd, dependent upon the configuration of the proposed generating facility. Portions of the Interconnection Customer's equipment may be energized when the associated testing for that portion has been completed and verified. The following is provided to serve as guidance and may or may not be prescribed in the Interconnection Customer's relay equipment inspection requirements.

| Relay Equipment | Testing Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Protection Device Function | Variable determined by relay type |
| Acceptance Testing | Test Document Review |
| Setting Calibration | Not specified |
| Tripping Check | Witness / Functionality |
| Sensing Devices | Test Document Review |
| Primary Current/Voltage | Witness / Functionality |
| Telemetry for Protection Scheme | Witness / Functionality |
