# PJM Wholesale Market Participation

Interconnection Customers that intend to participate in the PJM wholesale market should visit PJM New Service Requests for more information. The PJM Tariff (Manual 14A) can be obtained at: [http://www.pjm.com/](http://www.pjm.com/). Please visit Requirements for Transmission Interconnection for more information.

A PJM service request does not relieve an interconnection customer from the requirements of any applicable ICC interconnection rules.
