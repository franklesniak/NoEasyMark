## MODELING DATA FOR THE STEP-UP TRANSFORMER

1. TRANSFORMER WINDING DATA
   - A) RATINGS ONAN/ONAF/OFAF or similar (MVA)

2. TRANSFORMER IMPEDANCES GIVEN IN PER UNIT ON MVA BASE:
   - A) POSITIVE SEQUENCE RESISTANCE (pu)
   - B) NEGATIVE SEQUENCE RESISTANCE (pu)
   - C) ZERO SEQUENCE RESISTANCE (pu)
   - D) POSITIVE SEQUENCE REACTANCE (pu)
   - E) NEGATIVE SEQUENCE REACTANCE (pu)
