# Criteria for ComEd DER Interconnection

All DER interconnections will be evaluated for the following:

- Safety of the general public and ComEd personnel
- Risk of degradation to services for customers due to interruptions or power quality events
- Compromise of security or reliability of ComEd electrical systems

All owners of approved DER facilities are required to be responsive to ComEd direction and instructions during emergency conditions or to remove the DER from service when ComEd is performing work on the circuit to which the DER is connected. A DER customer is generally only permitted to operate while connected to ComEd feeder or line in the approved normal operating configuration. If the ComEd distribution system is out of normal operating configuration, the DER customer may be required to remain offline until the ComEd distribution system is restored to its normal operating configuration.

The following is intended to help guide the Interconnection Customer in designing a configuration that best supports the criteria for ComEd DER interconnection.

This document only covers distribution interconnections, and not transmission interconnections.
