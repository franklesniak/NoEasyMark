## Purpose

This document contains ComEd's official guidelines for customers
interconnecting distributed energy resources (DER) such as solar
photovoltaic systems and battery energy storage. It is a key reference
for:

- Understanding ComEd's DER interconnection process and requirements
- Solar and solar-plus-storage installation planning
- Meter and service equipment requirements for DER installations
- Coordination with ComEd for DER interconnection applications
