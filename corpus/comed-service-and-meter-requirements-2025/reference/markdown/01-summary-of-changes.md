# Summary of Changes in This Edition

## 2025-03-28 Update

| Number | Change |
| --- | --- |
| C9111 | Revised note 2.2 to include exceptions for cold sequencing. Added notes 2.2.1 and 2.2.2 to specify the conditions under which cold sequencing is permitted. Renumbered sections 2.5 and 2.6. Included a note in section 4.5 regarding meter clearance from windows. Updated references in section 5.12. |
| C9150 | Figure 2 has been updated to display the accurate configuration, with Z designated as the high leg. The wiring between the meter fitting and the CT compartment has been corrected in Figures 1-3. |

## 2025-06-27 Update

| Number | Change |
| --- | --- |
| C7973 | The option for the customer to have a pad mounted junction/isolation box was added to better reflect existing builds. The absence of a pole mounted junction box made the standard difficult to apply resulting in variant builds. This revision is meant to clarify that street light customers are to build to this standard if they want underground service from an overhead secondary. This applies as long as a junction/isolation box is present between the source and point of connection. |
| C7974 | Table-1 pre-approved fused wire was updated. Pre-approved vendor was revised to reflect market availability. |
| C9162 | Added dual meter for ethernet over IP without breakout block. Updated standard includes newer design that no longer requires breakout block and cellular data update. |

## 2025-09-26 Update

| Number | Change |
| --- | --- |
| C9110 | Note for window clearance added on page 6. Moved from reference in 4.5 of C9111 and should be captured with door clearances. Current note on window clearance can be interpreted as a radial clearance, and meters shall not be installed above, below, or behind any window on the first floor or lower. |
| C9111 | Revised gas meter note in 4.5 for more specific meter clearance and updated C9110 reference. Removed door and window notes from 4.5 and moved to C9110 page 6 notes. |
| C9140 | Updated compliance verbiage and removed "be approved by the Company prior to construction" for ITEM A. Instrument transformer cabinets are not individually reviewed and approved by ComEd. They must follow the construction requirements in C9140 that is located in ComEd's Service and Meter Requirements file. |
| C9141 | Updated compliance verbiage and removed "be approved by the Company prior to construction" for ITEM A. Instrument transformer cabinets are not individually reviewed and approved by ComEd. They must follow the construction requirements in C9141 that is located in ComEd's Service and Meter Requirements file. |
