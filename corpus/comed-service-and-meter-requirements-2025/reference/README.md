## Purpose

This document contains ComEd's official requirements for electrical service and metering installations. It is a key reference for:

- Meter socket selection and approval (relevant to DISC-003)
- Underground service conduit and conductor specifications
- Service entrance equipment requirements
- ComEd coordination procedures for new and upgraded services
